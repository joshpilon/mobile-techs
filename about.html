About Mobile Techs

Trusted by Lewiston since 2010, we’re here to fix, buy, and sell mobile devices with integrity and speed.

Our Story

Josh founded Mobile Techs with one goal: to make tech repair more personal, transparent, and reliable by providing the LC Valley and surrounding areas with exceptional device repair services.

With over 15 years in mobile device repair and IT consulting, Josh has served thousands of customers through honest advice, quick turnarounds, and high-quality parts. From cracked screens to motherboard micro-soldering, our experience runs deep.

We’re proud to be the tech team our neighbors trust—fixing what's broken, buying what's no longer needed, and helping you stay connected to what matters.

Why We Exist

Too often, tech repair shops are confusing, overpriced, or just plain unhelpful. We started Mobile Techs to flip that script—to create a space where customers feel seen, respected, and empowered in every interaction.

Whether you’re a parent with a toddler-busted iPad or a business owner in need of reliable tech support, our mission is the same: provide fast, fair, and friendly service that gets you back up and running.

A Look Back

2010 – Mobile Techs Launches

Josh opens Mobile Techs in Lewiston, Idaho with a folding table and a dream: to provide honest repair work with zero BS.

2014 – New Services Added

We expand beyond smartphones to offer tablet, computer, and game console repair.

2018 – Business IT Support

Mobile Techs begins offering managed IT services and tech support for small businesses in the LC Valley.

2023 – Storefront Remodel

With a fresh new space, modern tools, and a growing team, Mobile Techs levels up again to serve more customers than ever.
