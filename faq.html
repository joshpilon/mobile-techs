Repair Services

Most standard repairs like screen replacements, battery replacements, and charging port fixes are completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs might take up to 24 hours. We'll always provide you with an estimated completion time when you drop off your device.

We offer both original manufacturer parts and high-quality aftermarket parts. Original parts maintain your device's warranty but are more expensive. Our aftermarket parts are thoroughly tested for quality and compatibility, and come with our own warranty. We'll discuss your options and pricing before beginning any repair.

We repair most popular smartphones, tablets, and smartwatches, including all models of iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, and many other Android devices. We also repair iPads, Samsung tablets, and Apple Watches.

Additionally, we service:

  • Gaming consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo)
  • Gaming controllers
  • Laptops and computers

If you're unsure whether we can repair your specific device, please contact us with your device model information.

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait time. You can book an appointment through our website, by phone, or in person. For common repairs like screen replacements, we often have same-day appointments available.

Device Trade-in

We evaluate devices based on several factors: model, storage capacity, condition (cosmetic and functional), battery health, and current market demand. Our technicians perform a comprehensive assessment using diagnostic tools to verify the device's functionality and condition. We pride ourselves on offering competitive and fair prices based on real-time market values.

Before trading in your device, please:

  • Back up all your data (photos, contacts, etc.)
  • Sign out of all accounts (Apple ID, Google, social media)
  • Turn off Find My iPhone/Device Protection features
  • Remove your SIM card and memory card (if applicable)
  • Perform a factory reset
  • Clean your device (if possible)
  • Bring your charger and any accessories you want to include

We can assist with these steps in-store if needed, but having them done beforehand speeds up the process.

Yes, we accept devices with various types of damage, including cracked screens, battery issues, and water damage. While damage will reduce the trade-in value, we still offer fair prices for damaged devices. In some cases, we may suggest repairing the device first if that would significantly increase its value.

The only devices we cannot accept are those that:

  • Have been reported lost or stolen
  • Have unremovable account locks (iCloud, Google, etc.)
  • Have unrepairable motherboard damage

We offer several payment options for device trade-ins:

  • Venmo, Apple Pay, PayPal (available immediately)
  • Store credit (with a 10% bonus value)
  • Zelle Direct Bank Deposit (possible fee)

You'll need to provide a valid ID for all trade-ins, and we'll provide a detailed receipt documenting the transaction.

Warranty & Store Policies

All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering parts and labor. This warranty covers any defects in the parts we install or issues directly related to our repair work. If you experience any problems with the repaired component within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

The warranty does not cover:

  • Physical damage occurring after the repair
  • Water damage
  • Issues unrelated to the original repair
  • Unauthorized third-party repairs or modifications

All our certified pre-owned devices come with a 6-month warranty covering hardware defects and functionality issues. This comprehensive warranty ensures your refurbished device performs reliably.

Our refurbished device warranty includes:

  • Battery performance (maintains at least 80% capacity)
  • Screen functionality (touch response, display quality)
  • Camera operation
  • Button functionality
  • Speaker and microphone performance
  • Charging system

We also offer optional protection plans that extend coverage up to 24 months and include accidental damage protection.

Our return policy varies by product category:

  • Refurbished Devices: 14-day return period for full refund or exchange
  • New Accessories: 30-day return period (unopened) or 14-day return period (opened)
  • Repair Services: If you're not satisfied with a repair, please return within 7 days for adjustment

All returns require:

  • Original receipt
  • All included accessories and packaging
  • Device in same condition as purchased (for device returns)

Refunds are issued in the original payment method. Store credit refunds are available immediately, while credit card refunds may take 3-5 business days to process.

We take data privacy extremely seriously. Our privacy policy for repairs includes:

  • All technicians sign strict confidentiality agreements
  • We only access device data when necessary for testing functionality
  • Security cameras monitor all repair areas
  • Devices are stored in secure areas when not being worked on
  • We recommend backing up and erasing sensitive data before repairs when possible
  • We can assist with temporary data protection measures before repairs

If you have specific privacy concerns, please discuss them with our staff before your repair, and we'll accommodate special requests whenever possible.
